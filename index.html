Quick information on encryption

Encryption is a somthing used in our every day lives, wheather its sending an email or getting bank details encryption is always there to help protect your private information form other people. -
common uses of encryption are sending emails or texts, since you generally dont want people seeing what you send to other people encryption is there to help keep your messages private and away from other people.
encryption is an age old technique that has been developed over the years one of the most common forms of encryption that was made a long time ago was the enigma code, this was a german creation that allowed them to send secret messages through the war, it was however cracked so it wasnt the safest form of encryption, however encryption only got better from there.
