What are modern uses of encryption

Encryption is used everywhere you look nowdays. sending emails, saving passwords, logging in to websites, keeping your bank details safe or just keeping your technology private from the outside world.
This helps out alot with modern advancements in technology, say your working on some top secret message that cant be shared with anyone besides the receiver, thats when encryption comes into play, safely and securley sending your messages so outside eyes cant see it.
