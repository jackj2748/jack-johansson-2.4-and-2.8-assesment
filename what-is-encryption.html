What is encryption?

so What is encryption?

Encryption is a form of protection for your messages or "packages" as they are called online, its like armor to keep away any unwanted people from getting to your very very important stuff. Like your bank details or passwords for your email or other important things that you would normally want hidden in the real world. think of it like your putting your messages in a metal locked box locking the box and then sending it to someone else who has the key for the box so that only they can have access to the information that you send out.
